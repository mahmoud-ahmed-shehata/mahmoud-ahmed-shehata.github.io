Baniyas Car Showroom

Role: Project Engineer Scope: Structure · Steel · Finishing · Coordination · QA/QC · Drawings

Large-scale car showroom project combining reinforced concrete and steel structure, aluminium and glass façades, skylights, steel canopies and high-end interior finishing. The role required daily site supervision, resolving technical issues between architectural, structural and MEP works, and keeping approvals and quality aligned with the consultant and client expectations.

RC & steel structure Aluminium & glass façades High-end interior finishing RFIs · WIRs · MIRs
Key responsibilities & highlights
  • Daily supervision of structural and finishing works on site, including inspections and quality checks for concrete, block, plaster and finishing activities.
  • Coordinating between structural, architectural and MEP drawings to avoid clashes and resolve discrepancies before execution.
  • Raising RFIs for missing or unclear details and following up on consultant responses to implement approved solutions on site.
  • Managing WIRs/MIRs, ensuring works are inspected and approved prior to proceeding with subsequent activities.
  • Supervising façade interfaces (aluminium, glass and steel elements) and ensuring alignment with design intent and shop drawings.
  • Monitoring finishing quality for floors, walls, ceilings and showroom areas to match the required high-end standard.
  • Supporting short-term planning and sequencing of activities to keep critical path items and handover milestones on track.
