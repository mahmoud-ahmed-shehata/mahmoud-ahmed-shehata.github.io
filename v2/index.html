About
About Mahmoud

I’m a Civil / Project Engineer based in Abu Dhabi, focused on delivering clean, reliable site execution with a strong eye for finishing details and coordination.

Over the past 6+ years I’ve contributed to projects across the UAE, Egypt and Kenya – including car showrooms, private villas and residential towers – supporting both structural and finishing works on site.

  • • Comfortable managing drawings, RFIs, WIRs/MIRs, site inspections and short-term planning.
  • • Hands-on with subcontractors, consultants and suppliers to keep work moving smoothly on site.
  • • Focused on clear communication, safe execution and delivering work aligned with design intent.
