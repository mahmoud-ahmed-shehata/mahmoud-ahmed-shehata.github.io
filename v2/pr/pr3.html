PR3 – Baniyas Car Showroom

Role: Project Engineer Scope: Structure · Finishing · Coordination

Large-scale car showroom project with reinforced concrete and steel structure, aluminium and glass façades, solar shading elements and high-end interior finishing — requiring close coordination between structural, architectural and MEP works on site.

Project highlights
  • Daily site supervision for structural and finishing works, including inspections and quality checks.
  • Coordination between drawings on site: structural, architectural and MEP to avoid clashes and rework.
  • Managing RFIs, WIRs/MIRs and follow-up with the consultant for timely approvals.
  • Supervising façade works (aluminium & glass) and ensuring alignment with design intent and details.
  • Supporting short-term planning to keep activities moving in line with the overall project schedule.
