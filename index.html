About
About Mahmoud

I’m a Civil / Project Engineer based in Abu Dhabi, with 6+ years of experience in site execution, high-end finishing, structural coordination and QA/QC follow-up. I focus on delivering clean, well-coordinated work with clear reporting and strong day-to-day control on site.

I’ve contributed to commercial, residential and luxury villa projects across the UAE, Egypt and Kenya—supporting subcontractor coordination, shop drawing review, inspections (WIR/MIR), technical problem-solving and handover readiness.

My strength is bridging the gap between drawings and execution—ensuring details are built correctly, issues are closed quickly, and quality stays consistent from start to finish.
