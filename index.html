About
About Mahmoud

I’m a Civil / Project Engineer based in Abu Dhabi, with more than six years of experience in site execution, finishing, structural coordination and day-to-day project supervision. My approach is practical, detail-oriented and focused on delivering clean, well-coordinated work with high standards.

Throughout my career I’ve contributed to commercial, residential and luxury villa projects across the UAE, Egypt and Kenya — ensuring accuracy on site, resolving technical issues and maintaining smooth coordination between consultants, subcontractors and suppliers.

My strengths include high-end finishing supervision, structural strengthening, RFIs, WIRs/MIRs, shop drawing reviews, short-term planning and maintaining consistent quality and communication across the project cycle.
